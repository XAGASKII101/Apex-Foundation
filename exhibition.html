Competition Overview

Welcome to the APEX Science Exhibition Competition! This prestigious competition is open to high school students in West Africa, providing a platform to showcase their innovative science projects. Participants will compete for exciting prizes and recognition, with the opportunity to showcase their talents and passion for science.

The competition aims to inspire and motivate young minds to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). By participating, students will develop their problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity, essential for success in today's fast-paced world.
