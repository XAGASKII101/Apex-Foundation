APEX Foundation Maths Quiz

Get ready to put your math skills to the test! The APEX Foundation Maths Quiz is a prestigious competition open to high school students in West Africa. With a cash prize up for grabs, this is an opportunity you won't want to miss!

The quiz will cover a range of math topics, from algebra to geometry, and everything in between. Whether you're a math whiz or just looking to challenge yourself, this quiz is the perfect way to showcase your skills and compete with your peers.

The APEX Foundation Maths Quiz is designed to inspire and motivate high school students to excel in mathematics. The competition aims to identify and reward talented students who demonstrate exceptional math skills and problem-solving abilities.

By participating in the quiz, students will have the chance to:

  • 1. Showcase their math skills and knowledge

  • 2. Compete with other high school students in West Africa

  • 3. Win a cash prize and recognition

  • 4. Develop their problem-solving and critical thinking skills

  • 5. Gain confidence and motivation to pursue math-related fields

Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of the APEX Foundation Maths Quiz! Register now and take the first step towards showcasing your math skills and winning exciting prizes!
